About TukTukGo

Welcome to TukTukGo, your go-to platform for hassle-free tuk-tuk bookings! With TukTukGo, you can easily book tuk-tuks for your transportation needs without the fuss. Simply enter your pickup location and destination, and our platform will provide you with instant fare estimates and available tuk-tuks nearby. While our platform doesn't feature real-time location tracking of tuk-tuks at the moment, we strive to provide you with accurate and reliable service through our network of trusted tuk-tuk operators. Book your ride with confidence and experience the convenience of TukTukGo today!

Project Overview

Our project aims to streamline the tuk-tuk booking process by leveraging technology to enhance user experience. The application is designed for residents, tourists, and commuters seeking affordable and reliable transportation options in urban areas of India.

Technological Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Django & MySQL
  • Deployment: GitHub Pages

User Interface Design

Our user interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, ensuring a seamless booking experience. The application features responsive design, ensuring accessibility across different devices and screen sizes.

Booking Process

Users can easily book a tuk-tuk by entering their pickup location, destination, and preferred time. The application provides fare estimates and allows users to track their ride in real-time, ensuring a transparent and reliable booking experience.

Real-Time Tracking

Our application integrates with mapping APIs to provide real-time tracking of available tuk-tuks. Users can see nearby tuk-tuks on the map and make informed booking decisions, enhancing their overall transportation experience.
